Where Public Procurement meets Blockchain

The first blockchain-complete public procurement platform is now live!

The Ethereum-powered procurement platform is built to keep the middlemen out, to make sure the best bidder finds the best dealer, ensure that public data is truly public and common citizen's money isn't fiddled with.

The Fault in Our Roots

Plagued with bribery

The OECD 2014 report on foreign bribery says 57% of all bribes to foreign officials were to obtain public procurement contracts.

Collusions to bias the system

Due to the nature of competitive auctions there is a strong incentive for selfish anti-trust behaviour which manifests itself in legal and illegal ways.

Sluggish and inefficient

The public procurement process is far from a smooth one - involves delays, bullying and even organized crime.

Tender fixing

Contractors are coerced or treated unfairly during the tenure of the tender discouraging private ventures.

Code is law

Bidding using Ethereum smart contract auctions, documents on IPFS.

Everyone is watching

Using a public ledger as a rock-bed for all transactional evidence forces transparency onto officials and contractors keeps the procurement process auditable by third parties like journalists and government watchdog groups.

Open yet protected

Using homomorphic encryption and zero-knowledge proofs, we can audit and prove fairness of an auction while simultaneously holding back specific information about the bidders private until the auction is completed.

Core features preserved, Integrity and Usability enhanced

Wide compatibility

With most popular browsers from Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Brave supporting MetaMask, compatibility isn't a hurdle any longer.

Massively scalable

With Ethereum as the global network for computation and transaction processing, there are no more servers overloading and failing.

Autonomous transactions

With smart contracts governing transactions, justice to tenders is embedded immutably into the code.

Essentials without clutter

With clearly indicative UI, tender opportunities are not slipped away from the deserving.

Immutability

A transaction / bid / bill once confirmed is embedded into the blockchain and goes deeper every unit of time.

User-friendly

Where there is transparency, there is clarity.

Decentralized

Government and public bodies are auditors of themselves.
